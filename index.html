Transformer architecture from the video
📖
Component 1
Encoder — the reader
Scans the entire token sequence and builds a mathematical meaning map of the input.
🔦
Component 2
Attention — the spotlight
Focuses on the most relevant tokens — e.g. "colour" and "sky" — to understand the intent.
✍️
Component 3
Decoder — the writer
Generates the answer one token at a time: "The" → "sky" → "is" → "blue." Each token informs the next.
